About Me!

My name is Nikolas

I have decided to dedicate the personal page that has been given to me to talk a little bit about myself

My name is Nikolas Pantazoglou, I was born on the 25th of June, 2006, and I am currently 19 years old.

I was born and currently live in Greece, although I have temporarily lived on other countries, such as England (for 2,5 years) and Bulgaria (for half a year).

I studied veterinairy medicine in Bulgaria prior to my studies currently in Computer Science, however I had to stop attending that university due to multiple personal reasons.

I have always been fascinated by art, animation and videogames, so I know that I want my future job to revolve around one, if not all of these.

When I found out I can study computer science, I was pretty excited, knowing that I will learn to code and gain skills required to become a game developer.

Although that dream of mine looks sort of hard to achieve, I will chase it as much as I humanly can, because creating is my passion and I love making things for other people to enjoy.

Until then, I will continue to create no matter what circumstance. Whether that is drawing, editing videos, or even making websites such as this one.

In the near future, I hope to have all the necessary skills to put all my thoughts into a canvas.

Thank you for reading about me.
